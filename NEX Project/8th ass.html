Our Services

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department offers a variety of diagnostic
Cardiology procedures to determine underlying heart
Diseases and/or abnormalities and problems with the
Heart. These procedures help your doctor determine the
course of treatment to help you maintain a healthy and
productive life.



Physiotherapy

We provide the best physiotherapy/physical therapy and
rehabilitation to all ages with our multi-disciplinary team
that is experienced with a variety of health care needs.
Our treatment encompasses the present problems as
well as the injury prevention measures for posterity.



Paediatrics

The children outpatient unit host a variety of clinics.
These cover both general Paediatric and specialsit
Services.



Oncology

Delivering quality outpatient care to patients with all types of cancer.
Our services include:Outpatient Chemotherapy Administration Treatment of Blood Disorders
Comprehensive Consultation and Follow-up Care by Oncologist/Hematologist



Dermatology

We excel at diagnosing and treating conditions affecting
the skin, hair, and nails. Patients have trusted us for
years to receive the most advanced treatment in
medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology.



Labouratory Services

Havana Specialist Hospital's full-service, hospital-based
laboratory operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, serving both inpatient
and Emergency Department needs. Services are also extended to physicians' offices,
nursing homes, employers, outpatients, and homebound patients.



Gastroenteroloy

Gastroenterology essentially addresses diseases of the
esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon and rectum,
pancreas, gallbladder, bile ducts, and liver. We are
dedicated to the management of diseases of the
digestive and hepatobiliary diseases in both children
and adults.



Dietetic Services

Our dietitians provide dietetic counselling and nutritional
intervention to reduce the risk of diet-related diseases and
promote health through proper eating habits.



Radiology

The Imaging Department of Havana Specialist Hospital
is dedicated to offering our community a complete range
of diagnostic imaging procedures.
